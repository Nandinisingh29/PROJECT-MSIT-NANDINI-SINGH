MEDICAL ROOM


Description


A medical room has been specially formulated to help the students in case of any emergency. The college provides all kinds of first aid, medicines and other minor medical services as per the emergency needs. We make sure that First Aid Kit is always available.


In order to provide the First Aid in case of any medical urgency, the Institute has a well-equipped First Aid Room for the students & staff/faculty. It is the first port of call for any student who has sustained an injury or is feeling unwell. The First Aid Room is located on the Ground floor.
