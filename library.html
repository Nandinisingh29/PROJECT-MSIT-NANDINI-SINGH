LIBRARY


Description


Central Library of the MSIT College, Kolkata, is the nerve centre for all academic activities of the Institute. It is located at first floor of the Central Building, spreading over 3,700 sq feet. The library is partly air-conditioned and equipped with modern IT application. There are separate reading room for faculty and students. The sitting capacity of the reading room of the library is for 60 readers. Local Area Network (LAN) connection exists in the library including wireless and WiFi network throughout the library premises. Users are able to connect the library computers as well as their own devices through the library network.


The library has a rich collection of national /international books and journals in the field of Commerce, Arts & Science on different subjects including reference books. The subjects include ASPM, Bengali, Botany, Chemistry, Commerce, Computer Science, Economics, Education, Electronics, English, Environment, Geography, Hindi, History, Journalism, Mathematics, Philosophy, Physical Education,Physics, Physiology, Political Science, Sanskrit, Urdu & Zoology. Total collections including text books & reference books are more than 31 thousand up to financial year 2020-21.
